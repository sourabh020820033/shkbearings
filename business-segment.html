Industrial Bearings For Every Application

The Workhorses of Industry: Industrial Bearings for Every Application At SHK BEARINGS, we understand the critical role industrial bearings play in keeping the wheels of industry turning. From robust ball bearings to versatile spherical bearings and reliable pillow block bearings, we offer a comprehensive range of solutions to meet the demanding needs of various industrial applications.

Unrelenting Reliability: Ball Bearings for Everyday Industrial Use

Deep groove ball bearings: The go-to choice for general industrial applications, offering a balance of load capacity, speed, and simplicity.

Angular contact ball bearings: Ideal for applications requiring high axial loads and moderate radial loads, often found in gearboxes and pumps.

Self-aligning ball bearings: Accommodate shaft misalignment, making them perfect for applications with imprecise mounting or thermal expansion.

Adaptable Performance: Spherical Bearings for Diverse Applications

Rod end bearings: Offer articulation and misalignment capabilities, commonly used in linkages and control systems. Track rollers: Handle heavy radial loads and tilting moments, often seen in construction equipment and material handling applications.

Gimbal bearings: Provide two-axis angular motion, used in robotic arms, aerospace components, and scientific instruments.

Convenience and Durability: Pillow Block Bearings for Easy Mounting

Cast iron pillow block bearings: Economical and robust, suitable for moderate loads and speeds in general industrial applications.

Stainless steel pillow block bearings: Corrosion-resistant for harsh environments like food processing or chemical plants. Polymer pillow block bearings: Lightweight, self-lubricating, and quiet, ideal for applications requiring cleanliness and low maintenance.
